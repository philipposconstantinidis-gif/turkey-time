Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This policy explains how RoastTime ("we") handles information when you visit roasttime.com.

Information We Collect

RoastTime does not require accounts or personal information. All calculations happen in your browser. We automatically collect anonymous technical data (IP address, browser type, pages visited) for analytics only.

Cookies

We use analytics cookies and advertising cookies (Google AdSense). You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting the calculator.

Third-Party Advertising

This site uses Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your browsing. Opt out at Google Ads Settings. See Google's Privacy & Terms.

Data Storage

We store no personal data. All inputs are processed in your browser and never transmitted to us. The site uses HTTPS encryption.

Your Rights (GDPR)

UK/EU residents have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. Since we don't collect personal data through the site, these mainly apply if you've contacted us directly. Contact us via the contact page.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
